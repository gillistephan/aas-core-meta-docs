---A view is a collection of referable elements w.r.t. to a specific viewpoint of one
or more stakeholders.

<div>
  <p>
    Constraint AASd-064: If the semanticId of a View references a
    ConceptDescription then the category of the ConceptDescription shall be
    VIEW.
  </p>
  <p>
    Views are a projection of submodel elements for a given perspective. They
    are not equivalent to submodels.
  </p>
</div>
